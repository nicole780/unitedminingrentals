Rental & Ownership

Four ways to put equipment to work.

Every model is designed for mining companies, tunnelling contractors, and start-up operations that want to defer capital spend until positive cash flow is realised.

01

Rent & Return

One or more years, no commitment to purchase. Return the equipment at term end with full flexibility.

No commitment
02

Rent & Purchase

Rent with an option to purchase after a minimum one-year term. A percentage of rental payments is deductible from the pre-agreed purchase price.

Path to own
03

Rolling Replacement

Return equipment after a 3-year rental and replace with new equipment for another 3-year term, or a pre-agreed period.

Fleet refresh
