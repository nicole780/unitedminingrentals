About United Mining Rentals

Three decades underground

Rental & ownership opportunities for new and used mining equipment — built on deep industry experience, full OEM support, and an uncompromising approach to safety.

Experience

United Mining Rentals has over 30 years' experience in the mining and tunnelling industry. Through deep industry involvement we've built close partnerships with globally recognised OEMs, so you get the equipment of choice at the lowest possible total cost of ownership.

Support

Our full range of new mining and tunnelling equipment is backed by full factory warranty, technical support, OEM parts, and a network of local and regional service centres.

Safety

We treat everyone with respect and pay particular attention to safety and health. Nothing is more important to us than the safety, health and well-being of our own people and everyone we work alongside.

Working with you

United Mining Rentals puts new and used underground mining and tunnelling equipment into your operation on terms built around your cash flow. Whether you are a mining company, a tunnelling contractor or a start-up operation, we tailor a sales, rental or rent-to-buy package that lets you defer capital spend until production is flowing.

Factory-backed support

As an authorised service partner we service, rebuild and support your fleet to factory standard, backed by OEM parts and around-the-clock technical support. Our new-equipment range is built around Siton's full line of underground machinery, complemented by quality used equipment from the leading OEMs.

Part of the QME group

United Mining Rentals works alongside its sister company QME — Quarry & Mining Equipment — who have supplied complete mining and tunnelling solutions worldwide since 1985, operating from bases in Ireland, Canada and Australia. Together we bring decades of underground experience to every project. Visit QME ›
